Title: Delicious Foods of Pakistan
Author: [Shazia Mohtashim]
Target Audience: Food lovers, travelers, aur jo Pakistani culture jaanna chahte hain
Tone: Engaging, appetizing, informative (jaise dost bata raha ho)

Structure:
1. Introduction
2. Chapter 1: Punjab (Focus: Lahore)
3. Chapter 2: Sindh (Focus: Karachi)
4. Chapter 3: Khyber Pakhtunkhwa (Focus: Peshawar)
5. Chapter 4: Balochistan (Focus: Quetta)
6. Chapter 5: Gilgit-Baltistan & Azad Kashmir
7. Conclusion
8. Glossary + Suggested Places

Rules for Every Dish:
- Description (flavor, ingredients, recipe)
- History & Origin (kahan se aaya, Mughal or local tradition)
- Cultural importance (Usually eaten, festivals or daily)
- No full recipes (only info)
- Engaging language, readers feel hungry after looking at the image

Word Count Goal: whole book should be around 30,000–40,000 words (each chapter 5,000–7,000)
