# Detailed Book Outline: Delicious Foods of Pakistan

1. Introduction (1,500–2,000 words)
   - Pakistani cuisine ka overview aur diversity
   - Historical influences (Mughal, Persian, Central Asian, regional)
   - Provinces ki unique flavors

2. Chapter 1: Punjab – The Heart of Rich Flavors (Focus: Lahore)
   - Famous Dishes: Biryani, Nihari, Paye, Haleem, Pulao, Saag with Makai ki Roti, Takatak
   - Street food culture in Lahore

3. Chapter 2: Sindh – Spicy and Aromatic Delights (Focus: Karachi)
   - Famous Dishes: Sindhi Biryani, Nehari (Karachi style), Pallo Fish, Bhugal Gosht, Gol Gappay, Rabri
   - Seafood aur Muhajir influences

4. Chapter 3: Khyber Pakhtunkhwa – Bold and Hearty (Focus: Peshawar)
   - Famous Dishes: Chapli Kebab, Mutton Karahi, Kabuli Pulao, Tikka, Namak Mandi specials
   - Pashtun hospitality

5. Chapter 4: Balochistan – Simple and Robust (Focus: Quetta)
   - Famous Dishes: Sajji (chicken/lamb), Dampukht, Kaak bread, Rosh
   - Nomadic traditions

6. Chapter 5: Gilgit-Baltistan & Azad Kashmir – Mountain Treasures
   - Famous Dishes: Chapshuro, Harissa, Mumtu (dumplings), Walnut cake, local breads
   - Hunza longevity foods

7. Conclusion
   - Food mein unity despite diversity
   - Tips for experiencing Pakistani foods

8. Appendices
   - Glossary of terms
   - Suggested places to eat in each city
